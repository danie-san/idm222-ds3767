Evan Harris

About Me

Evan Harris is an experienced filmmaker who has been pursuing film since the seventh grade. Evan has experience in most areas of film including but not limited to producing, directing, cinematography, and editing. While he has wide experience, his passions lie in directing and post-production. As someone who has enjoyed going to the theater and watching movies his entire life, Evan dedicates himself to making his dream of working in film a reality and to one day inspire others through film the way film has inspired him
